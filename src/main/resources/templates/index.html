Welcome to the Employee Management Information System.

This system was designed to simplify and automate the process of personnel management in organizations of any size. It allows tracking of employees, managing work time, handling leaves and sick days, conducting performance evaluations, managing employee training, as well as controlling structural units and positions within the company.

The system is developed based on modern technologies and approaches, ensuring high performance, reliability, and data security. The system's interface is designed with user convenience principles in mind, allowing for quick access to necessary information and task execution.

As a system administrator, you have full access to all functional capabilities of the system, including managing users and their roles, as well as setting system parameters.

We hope that using this system will help you improve personnel management in your organization and increase its efficiency. If you have any questions or suggestions, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Thank you for choosing our system!
